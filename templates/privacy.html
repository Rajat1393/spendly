Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you create an account and use Spendly, we collect the following information:

  • Account information: your name and email address provided at registration.
  • Expense data: amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions you enter into the app.
  • Usage data: basic information about how you interact with the app (e.g. pages visited, actions taken) to help us improve the service.

We do not collect payment information, government IDs, or any sensitive personal data beyond what is listed above.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide and improve the Spendly service:

  • To display your expenses, summaries, and spending patterns within the app.
  • To authenticate you and keep your account secure.
  • To diagnose bugs and improve app performance.

We will never sell, rent, or share your personal or financial data with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored on secure servers. We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your data against unauthorised access, loss, or misuse.

You may delete your account at any time. Upon deletion, all personal data associated with your account will be permanently removed from our systems within 30 days.

We retain anonymised, aggregated usage statistics that cannot be linked back to any individual user.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly uses a small number of trusted third-party services to operate:

  • Hosting provider: to run the application and store data securely.
  • Google Fonts: to load the typefaces used in the interface. Google may log font requests; see Google's Privacy Policy.

We do not use third-party analytics, advertising networks, or tracking pixels.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out via the contact information available within the app.

We will respond to all privacy-related enquiries within 7 business days.
